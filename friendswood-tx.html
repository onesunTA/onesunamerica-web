About Friendswood, TX

Premium Roofing for
Friendswood's High-Value Homes

Friendswood straddles the Galveston–Harris County line, nestled between Pearland to the west, League City to the east, and the Clear Lake/NASA corridor to the north. It's one of the most sought-after addresses in the southeast Houston area, with homes averaging $400,000 to $600,000+ — reflecting a community of well-insured, discerning homeowners who demand quality from every contractor they hire.

In August 2017, Hurricane Harvey devastated Friendswood. Widespread flooding, wind damage, and falling debris left virtually no neighborhood untouched. Harvey's impact on roof systems here was severe — and often invisible. Many repairs made in the chaotic weeks after the storm used substandard materials or cut corners, creating latent vulnerabilities that surface only under the next major event.

Friendswood's proximity to NASA's Johnson Space Center and the Clear Lake basin means the local homeowner base is highly educated, thorough in their research, and expects detailed documentation. One Sun America provides exactly that: a written inspection report, photographic evidence, and transparent communication through every stage of the project — from initial inspection to final clean-up.

Neighborhoods we serve include West Ranch, Rancho Viejo, Clearwater Estates, and established areas along FM 518 and FM 2351. Whether you're managing an insurance claim from a recent storm or simply overdue for a full replacement, we bring the same standard to every Friendswood home.
