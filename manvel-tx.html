About Manvel, TX

Roofing Built for
Manvel's Fastest-Growing Communities

Manvel is one of the fastest-growing communities south of Houston, anchored in Brazoria County and rapidly expanding through master-planned developments like Rodeo Palms, Pomona, Mustang Crossing, and Masters at Manvel. Young families are choosing Manvel for its lower tax rates, larger lots, and quieter atmosphere — and new construction is booming across every price tier from $280K to $420K.

But new construction doesn't mean storm-proof. The builder-grade shingles standard on most new Manvel homes are typically Class 3 rated — adequate for basic coverage but not optimized for Brazoria County's documented 4–5 hail events per year. Upgrading to Class 4 impact-resistant shingles like GAF Timberline HDZ at the time of any replacement is one of the smartest financial decisions a Manvel homeowner can make — many Texas insurers offer 15–30% premium discounts for Class 4 roofs.

Hurricane Harvey (2017) hit Brazoria County hard, with widespread wind and water damage throughout Manvel and neighboring Iowa Colony. Even homes that appeared undamaged after Harvey may have compromised underlayment or hidden moisture intrusion that only surfaces years later. One Sun America provides thorough free inspections with written reports — not a quick walkover.

With HOA communities growing quickly in Manvel, material compliance matters. We bring product samples, can match existing neighborhood profiles, and communicate directly with HOA boards when documentation is required for approval.

Builder-Grade to Class 4 Impact-Resistant Upgrade After Hail Damage

Mejora de Tejas del Constructor a Clase 4 Resistentes al Impacto Tras Daño por Granizo

A Rodeo Palms homeowner contacted us after a spring hailstorm revealed the limitations of their builder-grade Class 3 shingles — installed just three years prior. The inspection documented widespread granule loss and impact marks across 30 squares. We worked with a Public Adjuster to secure full insurance approval for replacement with GAF Timberline HDZ Class 4 impact-resistant shingles. The homeowner's insurer subsequently applied a 20% premium discount for the Class 4 upgrade. Total project: 30 squares, insurance approved, completed in 1.5 days.

Un propietario de Rodeo Palms nos contactó después de una tormenta de granizo de primavera que reveló las limitaciones de sus tejas Clase 3 del constructor — instaladas apenas tres años antes. La inspección documentó pérdida generalizada de gránulos en 30 cuadros. Trabajamos con un Ajustador Público para obtener la aprobación total del seguro para el reemplazo con tejas GAF Timberline HDZ Clase 4. La aseguradora del propietario aplicó posteriormente un descuento del 20% en la prima por la mejora Clase 4.
