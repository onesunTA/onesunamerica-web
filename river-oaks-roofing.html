Techado de Lujo para
Residencias de River Oaks

River Oaks is one of the most prestigious neighborhoods in the United States. Developed in the 1920s, the area is home to some of the largest private estates in Texas — properties that range from $1.5 million to well over $10 million. Roofing a River Oaks home is not the same as roofing a standard suburban property. Many homes feature steep-pitched roofs, complex architectural details, skylights, dormers, and premium materials including metal, clay tile, and high-end architectural shingles. These systems require contractors with experience and the standards to match.

River Oaks es uno de los vecindarios más prestigiosos de los Estados Unidos. Desarrollado en la década de 1920, el área alberga algunas de las residencias privadas más grandes de Texas — propiedades que van de $1.5 millones a más de $10 millones. Techar una casa en River Oaks no es lo mismo que techar una propiedad suburbana estándar. Muchos hogares tienen techos de alta inclinación, detalles arquitectónicos complejos, tragaluces, buhardillas y materiales premium que incluyen metal, teja de arcilla y tejas arquitectónicas de alta gama. Estos sistemas requieren contratistas con experiencia y estándares a la altura.

Houston's hail corridor doesn't make exceptions for River Oaks. The neighborhood averages 4–6 significant hail events per year, and its mature tree canopy — while beautiful — creates added risk during storms. Falling branches, debris impact, and decades of leaf buildup in gutters and roof valleys accelerate deterioration even on the best-maintained properties. Many River Oaks homeowners discover hidden damage only after water begins penetrating the structure.

Muchos propietarios de River Oaks descubren daños ocultos solo después de que el agua comienza a penetrar la estructura.
