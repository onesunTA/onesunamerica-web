About Alvin, TX

Roofing That Understands
Alvin's Story

Alvin, birthplace of Hall of Famer Nolan Ryan, sits in Brazoria County's storm belt — roughly 25 miles south of Houston and directly in the path of Gulf Coast weather systems that produce the region's consistent hail and wind events. With a population of around 28,000 and home values ranging from $170,000 to $280,000, Alvin is a working community where homeowners need real answers, not sales pitches.

A significant portion of Alvin's housing stock dates from the 1960s through the 1990s — homes with original or once-replaced roofs that are now aging into their second or third replacement cycle. These roofs are often approaching the end of their functional life and are more vulnerable to hail and wind damage than newer materials. Many Alvin homeowners are surprised to learn that even a modest storm event qualifies their older roof for full replacement under their insurance policy.

Hurricane Harvey (2017) caused significant flooding and roof damage throughout Alvin. Because Alvin is a smaller community, many homeowners felt underserved in the immediate aftermath — larger Houston contractors prioritized higher-density areas. One Sun America makes the trip to Alvin because we know the value of a contractor who actually shows up and does the job right.

We serve neighborhoods throughout Alvin including Longwood, Hillcrest, the Alvin Town Center area, and the FM 1462 corridor. Whether you have a 25-year-old roof due for replacement or storm damage from last season's hail, we'll give you an honest assessment and a clear path forward.

25-Year Shingle Roof Full Replacement — Wind Damage Discovered During Inspection

Reemplazo Total de Techo de Tejas de 25 Años — Daño por Viento Descubierto Durante Inspección

An Alvin homeowner in the Longwood area reached out after noticing a small ceiling stain following a spring storm. The roof was 25 years old and the homeowner expected to hear it was "just old." Our inspection found more: significant granule loss across multiple slopes, soft spots indicating compromised decking, and wind-lifted shingles along the ridgeline — all claimable storm damage. We coordinated with a Public Adjuster and secured full approval for replacement with Owens Corning Duration shingles, 22 squares. Insurance covered wind damage discovered during inspection. The homeowner's deductible was their only out-of-pocket cost.

Un propietario de Alvin en el área de Longwood nos contactó después de notar una pequeña mancha en el techo tras una tormenta de primavera. El techo tenía 25 años y el propietario esperaba escuchar que "simplemente era viejo." Nuestra inspección encontró más: pérdida significativa de gránulos, puntos blandos que indicaban tableros comprometidos y tejas levantadas por el viento a lo largo de la cumbrera — todo daño de tormenta reclamable. Coordinamos con un Ajustador Público y obtuvimos aprobación total para reemplazo con tejas Owens Corning Duration, 22 cuadros. El seguro cubrió el daño por viento descubierto durante la inspección.
